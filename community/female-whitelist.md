# Female Whitelist & Verification

To protect our female community and keep things authentic, all female role requests go through a quick camera verification handled privately by our female staff team.

## How It Works

1. Open a **support ticket** and request the female whitelist.
2. A **female staff member** will assist you.
3. You will be asked to do a **quick camera verification** to confirm you are legit.

Once verified, you are granted the **Females role**, a **starter pack**, **female priority**, and access to any restricted channels meant for females only.

## Why We Do This

This process prevents impersonation, protects our safe spaces, and keeps things respectful for everyone in the city.

{% hint style="info" %}
All verifications are handled **privately and respectfully** by our female staff team. Your camera check stays in the ticket and is never shared. Thank you for understanding and keeping it real.
{% endhint %}

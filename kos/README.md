# Faction Wars (KOS)

These rules apply to all KOS matches and Wars in Southside Roleplay. They exist to keep wars fair, clipped, and clean for every gang involved.

{% hint style="danger" %}
Failure to follow any rule may result in a round loss, a War DQ, gang strikes, or bans depending on severity.
{% endhint %}

## War Format

* Wars are **First to 5**.
* All participants must bring their own guns, ammo, and drugs.
* Wars are allowed any day of the week.
* Teams have **1 hour to lock the ticket**.
* If Team 1 wins the OG and Team 2 wins the double, either even it out or play the full pot, where the winner takes all three wins.
* You must be in a KOS VC during any war, unless agreed upon otherwise.
* **1 sub** is allowed by default.

# Round Replays (RR)

A round may be replayed for any of the following reasons.

* Killed by a player using a spider.
* Killed by a ghost or chip.
* Server restart during the round.
* Invalid clip posted.
* No tracers.
* Killed out of bounds.
* Drug shooting.
* Radio shooting.
* Exploit shooting, meaning using emotes while shooting.

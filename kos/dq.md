# DQ Rules

## DQ Timers

* If the war is locked, the host is present, and the entire team is at the war map, a **10-minute DQ** may be started.
* If the match has not started, the DQ timer is **10 minutes**. The entire team must be ready and proof must be provided.
* After the first round has started, DQ may begin after **5 minutes**.
* DQ timers must be announced. Example: `DQ Started - Ends :05`.

## Automatic War DQ

* Caught cheating.
* FTP x2.
* Mass RDM.

## Crashes & Disconnects

* If a player crashes or F8 quits, they must screen share while loading back in.
* Failure to notify an admin why you F8 quit results in a Player DQ.
* A war admin must be present during the screen share.

{% hint style="warning" %}
Waiting in queue does not pause the DQ timer. Only the loading screen does.
{% endhint %}

## Banned Player Rule

If a player has been banned from another FiveM server for cheating, and the opposing team can provide proof, that player will be placed on Clip Watch.

{% hint style="info" %}
This only applies to cheating bans.
{% endhint %}

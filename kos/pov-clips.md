# POV & Clip Rules

## Requesting POV

* When requesting POV, you must post your death.
* Any POV must contain **5 seconds before** and **3 seconds after** death.
* Death POV is required when requesting any POV.
* The player who dies must be the one to call POV. Nobody else.
* You only get **one POV request per round**.
* POV must include all audio (Discord, in game, etc.).
* Clips must be a minimum of **30 FPS**.

## Providing POV

If POV is requested, the player has **5 minutes** to provide it by:

* Uploading the clip, or
* Joining VC and screen sharing if they do not have Nitro.

You have **2 minutes to retrim** after the first post.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Clip Watch:** if you are on Clip Watch, you may be asked to post at any time during the war. You are allowed one request without a death POV per KOS.
{% endhint %}

## Failure to Provide POV

| Situation | Result |
| --------- | ------ |
| Failure to provide POV | Automatic loss of that round |
| Failure to provide POV twice | Automatic game DQ and placement on Clip Watch |
| On Clip Watch | 1 hour to post all kills from the war, or the war is reverted |

## Clip Quality Requirements

* Tracers must be clearly visible. Default tracers do not count.
* Bitrate and FPS must be high enough to clearly see all actions (30 FPS or higher).
* Only NVIDIA or AMD clipping software is allowed.
* If you have Nitro, you must upload your clip. Screen sharing is not allowed.

{% hint style="danger" %}
Renaming clips results in a War DQ and placement on Clip Watch.
{% endhint %}

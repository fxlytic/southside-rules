# Fear RP Quick Reference

Fear RP is the heart of Southside Roleplay. Value your life like it is real. This page summarizes the combat scenarios from [Fail RP](fail-rp.md).

## The Golden Rule

> If a gun is pointed at you and you are outnumbered, you fear your life. You do not pull a weapon you were not already holding.

## Key Reminders

{% hint style="danger" %}
You cannot reach for a weapon if someone is already threatening you with one. If you are alone at night against three people at gunpoint, you cannot escape without dying. Talking while dead is Fail RP. In a drive-by, the driver cannot shoot, and the car must come to a complete stop first.
{% endhint %}

## Green Zones

{% hint style="warning" %}
No initiating in a Green Zone. No fighting in a Green Zone. No shooting out of a Green Zone. No selling drugs in a Green Zone. Saying "come out the Green Zone" is [OOC](ooc.md), because there are no Green Zones in real life.
{% endhint %}

# ⚖️ Fear RP Quick Reference

Fear RP is the heart of SouthSide. **Value your life like it's real.** This page summarizes the [Fail RP](fail-rp.md) combat scenarios.

## The Golden Rule

> If a gun is pointed at you and you're outnumbered, **you fear your life.** You do not magically pull a weapon you weren't already holding.

## Key Reminders

{% hint style="danger" %}
* You **cannot** reach for a weapon if someone is **already** threatening you with one.
* If you're **alone at night vs. 3 people** at gunpoint, you **cannot escape without dying**.
* **Talking while dead** is Fail RP.
* The **drive-by driver cannot shoot** — the car must come to a complete stop.
{% endhint %}

## Green Zones

{% hint style="warning" %}
* No **initiating** in a Green Zone.
* No **fighting** in a Green Zone.
* No **shooting out of** a Green Zone.
* No **selling drugs** in a Green Zone.
* Saying **"come out the Green Zone"** is [OOC](ooc.md) — there are no Green Zones in real life.
{% endhint %}

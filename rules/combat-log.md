# III. F8 / Combat Log

Combat logging is purposely leaving a scene or closing the game to avoid an unwanted outcome.

## The Rule

When engaged in active roleplay, you should not disconnect from the server until the scenario has completely ended.

If you are a victim of [RDM](rdm.md), [Fail RP](fail-rp.md), or any other invalid situation, you must remain in the scene and city until it is over, then make a report in game.

{% hint style="danger" %}
You cannot combat log because someone else broke the rules. Stay in the scene. You will be reimbursed if the other person violated a server rule.
{% endhint %}

## Crashes

If you crash, you must keep your crash file to prove that you actually crashed.

## Examples of Combat Logging

* F8ing or disconnecting while being robbed.
* F8ing or disconnecting during a chase.
* F8ing or disconnecting during any scene in general, or during an admin sit.

{% hint style="info" %}
Getting wronged is frustrating, but the system only works if you stay. Leaving the scene forfeits your reimbursement and may earn you a punishment of your own.
{% endhint %}

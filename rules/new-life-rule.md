# V. New Life Rule (NLR)

Once you die, you **cannot remember your past life** or the events that led up to your death for **15 minutes** after you get revived.

## The Rule

{% hint style="danger" %}
Within **15 minutes** of being revived, if you:

* Interact with the **same person / opp / enemy**, **or**
* Return to the **location of your death**,

...that is **breaking NLR** and will result in a **ban**.
{% endhint %}

## Being Reminded

* You **can** be reminded of what happened by a person who was at the scene.
* This interaction **must be clipped** and **must be roleplayed out**.

## Returning to the Area

A player may **not**:

* Return to the area of their death for at least **15 minutes**, **or**
* Attempt to retrieve lost possessions from involved players.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Think of it as a fresh start.** Your character has no memory of who killed them or why. Acting on that knowledge — even subtly — breaks NLR.
{% endhint %}

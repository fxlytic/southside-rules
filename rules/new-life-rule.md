# V. New Life Rule (NLR)

Once you die, you cannot remember your past life or the events that led up to your death for fifteen minutes after you are revived.

## The Rule

{% hint style="danger" %}
Within fifteen minutes of being revived, you break NLR if you interact with the same person, opp, or enemy, or if you return to the location of your death. Breaking NLR results in a ban.
{% endhint %}

## Being Reminded

You can be reminded of what happened by a person who was at the scene. That interaction must be clipped and must be roleplayed out.

## Returning to the Area

A player may not return to the area of their death for at least fifteen minutes, and may not attempt to retrieve lost possessions from involved players.

{% hint style="info" %}
Treat it as a fresh start. Your character has no memory of who killed them or why. Acting on that knowledge, even subtly, breaks NLR.
{% endhint %}

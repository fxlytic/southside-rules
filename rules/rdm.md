# VIII. Random Deathmatch (RDM)

**RDM** is killing another person **without a sufficient roleplay reason**. Unnecessary provoking falls under this rule as well. **Terrorism is strictly forbidden.**

## Revenge Killing

This rule **also includes revenge killing** — respawning after being executed and then killing (or attempting to kill) the person who just murdered you.

{% hint style="danger" %}
Your IC memory is **wiped** of everything leading up to your death. You must **forget your attacker**. See the [New Life Rule](new-life-rule.md).
{% endhint %}

## The 2-Minute Rule

{% hint style="warning" %}
**YOU MUST HAVE 2 MINUTES OF RP WITH SOMEONE BEFORE ROBBING OR KILLING THEM.**
{% endhint %}

The **only** exception: you may kill someone without 2 minutes of RP if **they initiate with you** — by aiming a gun or threatening your life. See [Initiation](initiation.md).

## Examples

* Walking up to someone and **shooting them in the head** with no backstory or roleplay behind it.
* Killing without the required **2 minutes of RP**.
* Unnecessary provoking that leads to a kill.

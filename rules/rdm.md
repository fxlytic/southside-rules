# VIII. Random Deathmatch (RDM)

RDM is killing another person without a sufficient roleplay reason. Unnecessary provoking falls under this rule, and terrorism is strictly forbidden.

## Revenge Killing

This rule also covers revenge killing, which is respawning after being executed and then killing, or attempting to kill, the person who just murdered you.

{% hint style="danger" %}
Your in-character memory is wiped of everything leading up to your death. You must forget your attacker. See the [New Life Rule](new-life-rule.md).
{% endhint %}

## The Two Minute Rule

{% hint style="warning" %}
You must have two minutes of roleplay with someone before robbing or killing them.
{% endhint %}

The only exception is if they initiate with you by aiming a gun or threatening your life. See [Initiation](initiation.md).

## Examples

* Walking up to someone and shooting them in the head with no backstory or roleplay behind it.
* Killing without the required two minutes of roleplay.
* Unnecessary provoking that leads to a kill.

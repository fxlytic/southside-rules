# Reimbursement Guide

Lost gear because someone broke a rule? You can be reimbursed, but only if your claim meets every requirement below. Invalid claims are denied.

## Requirements

{% hint style="warning" %}
You must upload a valid two minute continuous clip with audio on both ends.
{% endhint %}

* The clip must be continuous, not cut or edited, and at least two minutes long.
* Audio must be present on both ends.
* The clip must clearly show what you lost.
* You must be the victim.

## What Makes a Claim Invalid

{% hint style="danger" %}
You must not be breaking any server rule in your clip. Exploiting, TOS, FRP, NLR, and anything else count. If a rule is broken on your end, you will also be punished.
{% endhint %}

If you react, the clip is no longer valid. Reacting includes:

* Aiming your weapon.
* Shooting back.
* Initiating.
* Harassing.
* Any action that causes a reaction.

Being "in your head" and getting looted is not valid. You are not meant to be AFK in a roleplay server.

## Quick Checklist

| Requirement | Status |
| ----------- | ------ |
| Two minute continuous clip | [ ] |
| Audio on both ends | [ ] |
| Clearly shows what you lost | [ ] |
| You were the victim and did not react | [ ] |
| You broke no rules in the clip | [ ] |

If you cannot check every box, the claim will be denied.

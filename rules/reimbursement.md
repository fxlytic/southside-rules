# Reimbursement Guide

Lost gear because someone broke a rule? You can be reimbursed — but **only if your claim meets every requirement below.** Invalid claims are denied.

## Requirements

{% hint style="warning" %}
You must upload a **valid 2-minute CONTINUOUS clip** with **audio on both ends**.
{% endhint %}

* ✅ The clip must be **continuous** (not cut/edited) and at least **2 minutes** long.
* ✅ **Audio on both ends** is required.
* ✅ The clip must **clearly show what you lost**.
* ✅ You must be the **victim**.

## What Makes a Claim Invalid

{% hint style="danger" %}
**You must not be breaking ANY server rule in your clip** — exploiting, TOS, FRP, NLR, etc. If a rule is broken on your end, **you will also be punished**.
{% endhint %}

* ❌ **If you react, the clip is no longer valid.** Examples of reacting:
  * Aiming your weapon
  * Shooting back
  * Initiating
  * Harassing
  * Any action that causes a reaction
* ❌ **Being "in your head" and getting looted is not valid.** You are not supposed to be AFK in an RP server.

## Quick Checklist

| Requirement | Status |
| ----------- | ------ |
| 2-minute continuous clip | ☐ |
| Audio on both ends | ☐ |
| Clearly shows what you lost | ☐ |
| You were the victim (didn't react) | ☐ |
| You broke **no** rules in the clip | ☐ |

If you can't check every box, the claim will be **denied**.

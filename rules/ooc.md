# II. Out of Character (OOC)

Players are required to remain **in-character (IC) AT ALL TIMES**. Bringing information learned outside of character into roleplay is OOC — and sometimes [Metagaming](meta-gaming.md) — and is a **bannable offense**.

## What Counts as OOC

* **Threatening to report someone** is OOC.
* **Discussing things that happened IRL.**
* Text chat, VOIP (Voicebox), radio, phone, and commands like `/ME` are to be used **only for IC content**.
* **OOC videos or screenshots cannot be used as IC evidence.**

## Pausing Roleplay

{% hint style="danger" %}
Only **administrators** are allowed to pause RP or investigate rule breaches. You cannot pause RP yourself.
{% endhint %}

## Specific OOC Violations

* Saying **"come out the Green Zone"** is considered OOC. Your character is meant to be like real life — there are **no Green Zones in real life**.
* Calling someone a **gender other than what they appear as in game** is considered OOC and is **ban worthy**.

{% hint style="info" %}
Treat everything your character knows as something they learned **in the city**. If you only know it because of Discord, a stream, or real life — your character doesn't know it.
{% endhint %}

# II. Out of Character (OOC)

Players are required to remain in character at all times. Bringing information learned outside of character into roleplay is OOC, and at times [Metagaming](meta-gaming.md), and it is a bannable offense.

## What Counts as OOC

* Threatening to report someone is OOC.
* Discussing things that happened in real life.
* Text chat, VOIP (Voicebox), radio, phone, and commands like `/ME` are for in-character content only.
* OOC videos or screenshots cannot be used as in-character evidence.

## Pausing Roleplay

{% hint style="danger" %}
Only administrators may pause roleplay or investigate rule breaches. You cannot pause roleplay yourself.
{% endhint %}

## Specific OOC Violations

* Saying "come out the Green Zone" is OOC. Your character lives like a real person, and there are no Green Zones in real life.
* Calling someone a gender other than the one they appear as in game is OOC and is ban worthy.

{% hint style="info" %}
Treat everything your character knows as something they learned inside the city. If you only know it from Discord, a stream, or real life, your character does not know it.
{% endhint %}

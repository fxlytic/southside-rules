# IX. Initiation

Initiation is the action that justifies a response. The following acts count as initiating against another player.

* Stealing someone's car.
* Threatening someone's life.
* Aiming. Aiming is initiation. You do not aim your gun at someone you do not intend to shoot.

{% hint style="warning" %}
If you aim at someone, you have initiated. Be ready for the consequences, and do not aim at people you have no intention of shooting.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="info" %}
Initiation is the exception to the [two minute roleplay rule](rdm.md). If someone initiates on you by aiming or threatening your life, you may respond without two minutes of prior roleplay.
{% endhint %}

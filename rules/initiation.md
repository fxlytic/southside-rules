# IX. Initiation

**Initiation** is the action that justifies a response. These acts count as initiating against another player:

* **Stealing someone's car.**
* **Threatening someone's life.**
* **Aiming.** Aiming is initiation — you do **not** aim your gun at someone you don't intend to shoot.

{% hint style="warning" %}
If you **aim** at someone, you've initiated. Be ready for the consequences — and don't aim at people you have no intention of shooting.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="info" %}
Initiation is the **exception** to the [2-minute RP rule](rdm.md). If someone initiates on you (aims or threatens your life), you may respond without 2 minutes of prior RP.
{% endhint %}

# IV. Fail RP (FRP)

**Fail Roleplay (FRP)** is when a person fails to roleplay properly — whether by going down an unrealistic route or intentionally disrupting roleplay by trolling.

## ⚖️ Fear RP — Valuing Your Life

You must **fear and value your life** when there is a gun pointed at you, especially when outnumbered.

> **Example:** If you are alone at night and 3 individuals hold you at gunpoint, you **cannot escape without dying**. You must follow Fear RP.

| Scenario | What You Can Do |
| -------- | --------------- |
| **1v1** | You may try to defend yourself **only if your weapon is already out**. You **cannot** reach for a weapon if the other person is already threatening you with one. |
| **2v1** | If you don't have a weapon out, you may try to **escape** (if not held at gunpoint). You **cannot** reach for a gun to protect yourself if they have one out. |
| **3v1** | You **must fear your life** if any weapons are out. You **cannot escape or defend yourself** unless you already have a weapon out. |
| **Equal (2v2, 3v3)** | You may defend yourself or escape — do whatever — but you **risk dying**. |

➡️ See the [Fear RP Quick Reference](fear-rp-reference.md) for a clean summary.

## Other Fail RP Violations

* Initiating in a **Green Zone**.
* Fighting in a **Green Zone**.
* Killing/robbing **without 2 minutes of RP**.
* **Talking while dead.**
* In a **drive-by** scene, the **driver cannot shoot**. The car must come to a **complete stop** for the driver to shoot.
* Knowingly **stealing an EMS or PD vehicle**.
* **Fighting while pregnant** in game.
* **Killing or shooting a pregnant female** in game.
* **Selling drugs in a Green Zone.**
* **Being "in your head"** when around or discovered by other players.
* **Popout RP.**

## Kidnapping

If you want to kidnap someone, you must **go behind them and tie them up**. Doing it unrealistically results in a **ban**. You must use `/me` and `/do`:

> `/me ties male's hands together tightly.`

## Crutches

* **No shooting when on crutches.** Results in an **FRP ban**.
* When put on crutches, **wait the time out**. Caught doing `/reloadskin` to avoid it = **FRP ban**.

## Hospital & PD

* **No shooting in front of or into the hospital.** Results in a **ban**.
* You **cannot shoot at PD** unless they have **shot at you first**. Non-lethals (tasers) **do not count**.
* You **cannot pick up your friends or drive away when you are cuffed**.
* You **cannot take weapons or equipment from PD**. **You cannot loot/rob them at all.**

## Gendered Items

* Males **cannot use female weapons**, but **can rob them off females to resell**.
* Males **cannot sell** female items like **wigs or designer bags**.

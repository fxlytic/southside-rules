# IV. Fail RP (FRP)

Fail Roleplay, or FRP, is when a person fails to roleplay properly. This covers going down an unrealistic route and intentionally disrupting roleplay by trolling.

## Fear RP and Valuing Your Life

You must fear and value your life when a gun is pointed at you, especially when you are outnumbered. If you are alone at night and three people hold you at gunpoint, you cannot escape without dying. You must follow Fear RP.

| Scenario | What You Can Do |
| -------- | --------------- |
| 1v1 | You may defend yourself only if your weapon is already out. You cannot reach for a weapon if the other person is already threatening you with one. |
| 2v1 | If you do not have a weapon out, you may try to escape when you are not held at gunpoint. You cannot reach for a gun to protect yourself if they have one out. |
| 3v1 | You must fear your life if any weapons are out. You cannot escape or defend yourself unless you already have a weapon out. |
| Equal (2v2, 3v3) | You may defend yourself or escape, but you risk dying. |

See the [Fear RP Quick Reference](fear-rp-reference.md) for a clean summary.

## Other Fail RP Violations

* Initiating in a Green Zone.
* Fighting in a Green Zone.
* Killing or robbing without two minutes of roleplay.
* Talking while dead.
* In a drive-by scene, the driver cannot shoot. The car must come to a complete stop for the driver to shoot.
* Knowingly stealing an EMS or PD vehicle.
* Fighting while pregnant in game.
* Killing or shooting a pregnant female in game.
* Selling drugs in a Green Zone.
* Being "in your head" when around or discovered by other players.
* Popout RP.

## Kidnapping

To kidnap someone, you must go behind them and tie them up. Doing it unrealistically results in a ban. You must use `/me` and `/do`, for example:

> `/me ties male's hands together tightly.`

## Crutches

* No shooting while on crutches. This results in an FRP ban.
* When put on crutches, wait the time out. Using `/reloadskin` to avoid it results in an FRP ban.

## Hospital and PD

* No shooting in front of or into the hospital. This results in a ban.
* You cannot shoot at PD unless they have shot at you first. Non-lethals such as tasers do not count.
* You cannot pick up your friends or drive away while you are cuffed.
* You cannot take weapons or equipment from PD. You cannot loot or rob them at all.

## Gendered Items

* Males cannot use female weapons, but they can rob them off females to resell.
* Males cannot sell female items such as wigs or designer bags.

# VI. Meta Gaming

Meta gaming is using any out-of-character information for in-character use.

## What Counts as Meta Gaming

* Using any OOC information for in-character purposes.
* Being in a call with someone on Discord, or any voice app, while playing the server.
* Using OOC chat or PMs to share your location, especially during scenes or while you are dead.
* Using out-of-game communications such as Discord, Skype, or ooVoo to talk in character while in game.
* Using `/pm` to communicate with another party while in a scene.

## Stream Sniping

Going into a server owner's stream, or anyone's stream while they are in game, to find their location and travel to them is stream sniping, and it is forbidden.

{% hint style="warning" %}
If your character would not know it, you cannot act on it. Discord calls, streams, and OOC chat are not sources of in-game knowledge.
{% endhint %}

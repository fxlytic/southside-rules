# VI. Meta Gaming

**Meta gaming** is using any **out-of-character information** for **in-character use**.

## What Counts as Meta Gaming

* Using any **OOC information** for IC purposes.
* Being in a **call with someone on Discord** or any voice app while playing the server.
* Using **OOC chat or PMs** to tell your location — especially during scenes or while you are dead.
* Using **out-of-game communications** (Discord, Skype, ooVoo, etc.) to communicate **in character** while in-game.
* Utilizing `/pm` while in a scene to communicate with another party.

## 🚫 No Stream Sniping

Going into a **server owner's stream** — or **anybody's stream** who is in-game — to find their location and go to them is **stream sniping** and is forbidden.

{% hint style="warning" %}
If you wouldn't know it as your **character**, you can't act on it. Discord calls, streams, and OOC chat are **not** sources of in-game knowledge.
{% endhint %}

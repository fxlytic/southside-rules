# I. Character Creation

Your character is your identity in Southside Roleplay. Put effort into it.

## Rules

* Character names must be realistic and resemble real world names.
* Your character cannot be a default. It must have some customization.
* Character names must follow TOS guidelines.
* No troll characters. Low effort characters will be asked to change, and this can result in an FRP ban.
* Females must be on female peds and males must be on male peds.

{% hint style="info" %}
We are LGBTQ friendly, and that roleplay is allowed. The ped matching rule is about realism, not identity.
{% endhint %}

## Clothing Restrictions

No "100k or die" clothing is allowed. This includes face paint, hockey or troll masks, black eyes, no hand, bare feet, and colored tattoos.

{% hint style="warning" %}
Low effort or troll characters can lead to a forced change and an FRP ban. When in doubt, keep it realistic.
{% endhint %}

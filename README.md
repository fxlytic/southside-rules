# 🌴 Welcome to SouthSide

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/southside-logo.png" alt="SouthSide Roleplay"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Welcome to the City

Welcome to **SouthSide Roleplay** — a streamer-friendly, story-driven FiveM community. These rules exist to keep roleplay immersive, fair, and fun for everyone. **Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse.** By joining the server, you agree to read, understand, and follow everything in this book.

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Read everything.** Not knowing a rule will **not** save you from punishment. If you're ever unsure about a situation, ask a staff member **before** you act — not after.
{% endhint %}

## The SouthSide Standard

* 🎭 **Stay in character at all times.** The city is your real life.
* 🎥 **Always be clipping.** No clip, no reimbursement, no report.
* 🤝 **Respect everyone.** Zero tolerance for hate speech and toxicity.
* 🔫 **Earn your action.** 2 minutes of RP before any rob or kill.
* 💀 **Value your life.** Fear RP is the heart of this server.

## How to Use This Book

Use the menu on the left to jump to any rule section. Each page covers one topic with clear examples of what **is** and **isn't** allowed. Start with the [General Server Rules](rules/general-rules.md) and the [Fear RP Quick Reference](rules/fear-rp-reference.md) — those two will keep you out of most trouble.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Need to report a rule break or request reimbursement?** Head to the [Reimbursement Guide](rules/reimbursement.md) and follow it exactly. Invalid clips get denied automatically.
{% endhint %}

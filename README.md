# Welcome to Southside Roleplay

Southside Roleplay is a streamer friendly, story driven FiveM community. This book is the full rulebook for the city. Everything you need to stay in character, stay legit, and keep the roleplay alive is here.

Reading these rules is part of joining. By loading into the server you agree to follow every rule in this book.

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Not knowing a rule will not protect you from a punishment.** If a situation is unclear, ask a staff member before you act, not after.
{% endhint %}

## The Southside Standard

These five principles sit behind every rule in the book.

* **Stay in character at all times.** The city is your real life.
* **Always be clipping.** No clip means no reimbursement and no report.
* **Respect everyone.** Hate speech and toxicity carry zero tolerance.
* **Earn your action.** Two minutes of roleplay before any robbery or kill.
* **Value your life.** Fear RP is the heart of the city.

## How to Use This Book

The menu on the left holds every rule, grouped by topic. Each page covers one subject with clear examples of what is and is not allowed.

If you are new, start with the [General Server Rules](rules/general-rules.md) and the [Fear RP Quick Reference](rules/fear-rp-reference.md). Those two pages cover the situations players run into most.

{% hint style="info" %}
Need to report a rule break or request your gear back? The [Reimbursement Guide](rules/reimbursement.md) walks you through it. Invalid clips are denied, so read it before you submit.
{% endhint %}

***

Rules are subject to change at staff discretion. Final interpretation of any rule rests with management.
